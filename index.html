Agriculture Technology

We at WODRF , we have realized that increasing meaningful employment opportunities along the agricultural value chain can give rural youth and young women the chance to engage in productive work and overcome social challenges. An often overlooked area of opportunity is the ‘green economy’ (e.g., solar energy, organic agriculture), which has the potential to become a growth sector for rural communities. We again found evidence that agriculture’s position as a growth sector in many rural communities could be a worthwhile option to provide young people opportunities within the value chain. Although, most rural youth and young women are already engaged in informal agriculture in some way, they may not see it as an attractive or viable career option given such obstacles as geographic isolation, unfriendly land use policies, poor infrastructure, unfriendly economy, and unavailable agricultural inputs.
